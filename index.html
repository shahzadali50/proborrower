Repair Options

1.

Bring Your Device To A Store Or One Of Our Approved Partners.

Visit your local Service Centre or approved partner store. We aim to repair most issues whilst you wait in store, these are mostly completed within 15-30 minutes. If this is something that can not be done we will advise upfront of your repair cost and expected time of completions.

2.

Book A Service Vehicle To Your Location.

We have a network of approved engineers to complete the repair at your desired location.
Our mobile service vehicles are built and designed with convenience in mind, with specifically designed mobile workshops we can offer over 80% of repair service at the roadside.

3.

Post Your Device To Repair HQ.

If you would like a simple mail order repair service, you can post your device to your head Service Centre. We aim to complete and dispatch each repair back to you, the customer on the same day we receive.
